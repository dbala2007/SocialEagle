## Additional Weight Loss Tips

* Walk **30–45 minutes** daily, aiming for **8,000–10,000 steps**.
* Include **20–30 minutes of strength training** 3–4 times per week to preserve muscle while losing fat.
* Finish dinner **2–3 hours before bedtime**.
* Prefer steaming, boiling, grilling, or sautéing with **1–2 teaspoons of oil per meal**.
* Choose whole grains such as **millets, brown rice, or red rice** more often than polished white rice.
* If you have diabetes or prediabetes, pair carbohydrates with protein (dal, sprouts, low-fat curd, paneer) and vegetables to help reduce blood sugar spikes.

Following this meal plan consistently, together with regular exercise and good sleep, should support sustainable weight loss while providing balanced nutrition in a familiar South Indian vegetarian style.
